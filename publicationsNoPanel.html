To find my citation report, etc, in Web of Knowledge, one first needs to go to (Subscription-only) Web of Knowledge, click on the Web of Science tab, click on Advanced Search, in the textarea enter

and press Search. A link to the results will be located at the bottom of the page. Following this link will show a list of my papers. One can finally select Create Citation Report on the right. But see Note below.
