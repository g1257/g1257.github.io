Features

  • Generic DMRG engine: isolate models and geometries
  • Local and SU(2) symmetries: decrease your computation time
  • Configurable finite-algorithm sweeps: sweep the lattice in a flexible way
  • Post-processing of observables: run once, measure any number of times
  • Concurrency support: run with MPI, pthreads or simply serially
  • FullPartial checkpointing support (*): re-start your runs
  • Testsuite: when adding feature X don't break feature Y
  • Time Evolution: compute time-dependent observables with state-of-the-art techniques
  • Temperature Dependence: Use the White's METTS algorithm
  • Spectral Functions: Use Different Dynamics methods including Correction Vector
  • Oracles: Gauge your accuracy with pure Lanczos and other auxiliary codes to match (use the same input)

(*) See issue 14 and issue 11
